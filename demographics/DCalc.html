The Dominion of Donorion

Kingdom Age: years
Physical Area The Dominion of Donorion covers an area of 122 thousand square miles. Of this, 70% (86 thousand sq. miles) is arable land, and 29% (35 thousand sq. miles) is wilderness.
Population The Dominion of Donorion has a total population of 14 million people.
Settlements The largest city has a population of 53 thousand people, the second largest 26 thousand. There are 4 other cities of note in the kingdom, and 84 towns. The remaining population lives in numerous small villages, isolated dwellings, etc.
Castles The Dominion of Donorion has 292 active castles and 111 ruined. Of these, 190 castles and 95 ruined are in civilized lands, and 102 castles and 16 ruined are in the wilderness, along borders, etc.
